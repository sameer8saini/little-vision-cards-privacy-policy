Privacy Policy
High Contrast Baby Flash Cards

Last updated: 24 Nov 2025

High Contrast Baby Flash Cards (“the App”) provides high-contrast visual stimulation images for newborns, infants, and toddlers. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle user data. We do not collect any personal information.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect or store personal information of any kind, including:

  • Names, email addresses, or contact information
  • Photos, videos, audio, or camera data
  • Location (GPS) information
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Cookies or analytics data
  • App usage data or crash reports

2. App Functionality

The App works completely offline and does not require any login or account. No data is transmitted to servers or shared externally.

3. Third-Party Sharing

Because the App does not collect data, it does not share data with any third parties.

4. Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

The App is designed for use by young children under adult supervision. We comply with COPPA by collecting no data from children under 13.

5. Permissions

The App does not request any sensitive device permissions. It does not access the camera, microphone, contacts, location, or storage beyond normal app caching.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. Any updates will be reflected in the “Last updated” date.
